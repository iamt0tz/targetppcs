About Us

Target Professional Pest Control Services is an independent local service provider with selected, reliable, professional and well-trained employees and technicians. It is managed by Ruel who have been in the industry for 30 years, and is a licensed Exterminator and Fumigator by the Fertilizer and Pesticides Authority. He handled various customers from food manufacturing companies, hotels & resorts, industrial & commercial establishments and residential houses in Cebu, Visayas and Mindanao.

Target Professional Pest Control Services provides environmental-friendly and eco-effective pest control services to its customers and is proud to use Integrated Pest Management (IPM) pest control practices which includes, among others, inspection and identification of pests-related problems and application of combined biological, mechanical and physical approaches.

In adherence to the requirements of the local Fertilizer and Pesticide Authority (FPA), Target Professional Pest Control Services also addresses the safety aspect in delivering quality pest control services. Each technician is provided with safety gadgets and preliminary safety precautions are observed before any service engagement starts.

Target Professional Pest Control Services is also an accredited Exterra user. Exterra is a Termite Baiting System, which is a patented product from the USA. This termite management method is only being used by selected Pest Control Operators and this is widely being used in the US, Asia and other tropical countries.
